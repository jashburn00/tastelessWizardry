How does this work?

You are a wizard exploring a cave in search of an ancient, very powerful, and very valuable treasure. There are undoubtedly monsters and other foul creatures ahead! You will have to defeat each one in order to move on.

Each time you defeat a monster, you will level up and gain the following effects:

  • Increased maximum health

  • Increased maximum mana

  • Replenished health and mana

Each time you defeat a monster, you will be given a number of random loot drops dependent on how strong the monster was. These drops can be:

  • a weapon

  • a spell

  • a set of armor

Mana recharges during battle, but beware; each monster you fight will be stronger than the last!
